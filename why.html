Why Universal Design?

Everyone Benefits

Legal Responsibility

Many countries enforce accessibility standads in physical spaces and digital media. In the United States this includes:

  • The Americans with Disabilities Act
  • Department of Justice Ruling for Title II of ADA which states that all web and mobile app content must meet WCAG 2.1 level AA standards.

In additional to legal responsibility, including accessibility and utilizing Universal Design increases customer loyalty and improves the reputation of the company.

Technology Must be Accessible

In modern society, essential services are often digital:

  • Healthcare portals
  • Government services
  • Banking systems
  • Job applications

Designing inaccessible systems means people are prevented from excercising basic rights. Universal design ensures that technology doesn't widen inequality - it helps eliminate it.
